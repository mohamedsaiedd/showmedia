about

Show Media Production, founded in 2020 and based in Egypt is an artistic and media production implementation company, and participated in the implementation of more than 100 projects, from films, series, theater, advertising and awareness campaigns, to organizing celebrations and events. The company's vision is to build a wide range of local and international customers. "Show Media Production" acts as a gateway to the implementation of film production, television and media production, The company also aims to provide a high level quality to commensurate with our clients expectations and their budget, and gain clients satisfaction and trust, We have a professional academic team of integrated filmmakers to facilitate the production process, whatever the volume of the production.
